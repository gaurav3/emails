Dear {{{{raw}}}}{{FIRST_NAME}}{{{{/raw}}}},


We are delighted to announce a special program at Ashram!

After a gap of three years, Sadhguru will be conducting "In the Lap of the Master" – a Special Sathsang at the Isha Yoga Centre - Coimbatore, on 17th and 18th July 2016. This is a precious opportunity for you to spend two eventful days at Ashram in the presence of Sadhguru.

The last time "In the Lap of the Master" was held, Sadhguru guided all the participants through powerful meditations and processes. For many, it was an unforgettable experience.

You can catch a few glimpses of a similar event held in 2009, when over 2,000 participants gathered at the Isha Yoga Center to be in the grace of Sadhguru. It was unprecedented. Those who embraced the opportunity were given special practices during the day and participated in sathsangs with the Master in the evening.

In fact, it was during those two exhilarating days that Sounds of Isha premiered the songs that now make up the mesmerizing "In the Lap of the Master" Album. To listen to the songs, please click here.

The upcoming July 2016 Special Program will be residential and conducted in English. Participants will need to arrive by 9 am on Sunday, July 17, and can leave after the program closes at  10.30 pm on Monday, July 18.

The Special Program is open only to those who have participated in an Inner Engineering Program. There is no registration donation for this program. However, you are welcome to contribute, to help us meet the expenses of organizing such an event.

Online registration will open shortly. Meanwhile, please fill the following "Expression of Interest" Form if you want a reminder once registrations open. This information will enable you to save the dates and plan your travel.

{{> spacer-white}}

  • Guru Pournami Celebration - July 19
  • Bhava Spandana Program (Ladies - English) - July 21 to 24
Do share this wonderful opportunity to be with Sadhguru at Ashram!
