Programs in Singapore July 2016 {{> drop-shadow }}

Hatha Yoga is about creating a body that will not be a hurdle but a stepping stone for you to blossom into your ultimate possibility.

- Sadhguru

We are happy to announce that, as a part of International Day of Yoga, we will be offering a series of Hatha Yoga programs in Singapore!

Isha Hatha Yoga programs are an unparalleled opportunity to learn practices derived from a yogic tradition maintained in its full purity and vibrancy for thousands of years. The classes will be conducted by Isha Hatha Yoga school trained teachers and will offer classical Hatha Yoga in its full depth and dimension.

In this article, Sadhguru introduces us to the phenomenal science of hatha yoga, corrects many misunderstandings about the process and explores how we can reap the maximum benefits hatha yoga has to offer.

{{> drop-shadow }} Hatha Yoga Programs {{> spacer-white}}

We are offering the following programs on various dates and timings throughout the month of July. Please click on the individual program icons for the list of program dates.

Surya Kriya - Fire up the Sun Within

Surya Kriya is a potent yogic practice of tremendous antiquity, designed as a holistic process for health, wellness and complete inner wellbeing.

Dates: Jul 4 to 6, Jul 11 to 13, Jul 16 & 17

Angamardhana - The Ultimate Fitness Session

Angamardhana is a system rooted in yoga, that offers to everyone the opportunity to invigorate the body and to reach peak physical and mental health.

Dates: Jul 2 & 3, Jul 18 to 21

Bhuta Shuddhi - Cleanse the Five Elements

The basis of all creation, including the physical body, is the five elements - earth, water, wind, fire and space. The wellbeing of the body and mind can be established by purifying these five elements within the human system.

Dates: Jul 2, 3, 7, 16, 17 and more

Nada Yoga - Power of Sound

Nada Yoga uses the power of sound to activate a particular kind of energy in different parts of the body, bringing a lasting sense of inner pleasantness. You will be learning first 3 verses of Nirvana Shatakam.

Dates: Jul 23, 24

Eye Care

Eye Care is a special program devised to handle eye problems, namely myopia (nearsightedness) and hyperopia (farsightedness). The practices strengthen the eye's overall vision and focus and maintain that even into old age.

Dates: Jul 30

Yoga for Attention & Memory

Set of practices that bring the necessary balance and clarity in your body and mind so that attention and memory function at their peak, enhancing your perceptive and cognitive abilities.

Dates: Jul 23, 24
